## Account & Sign-in
▶ Why not username+password?
Token-based login is safer (time-limited, scoped).

## Push & Notifications
▶ Not receiving pushes?
Check app permission/system notifications/Do Not Disturb/network; on iOS enable Banners.

## Billing & Tokens
▶ Refunds?
No refund after subscription cycle starts; unused tokens refundable within 7 days.
