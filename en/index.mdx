## Welcome to Openstrat

Openstrat is an AI-driven quantitative platform for US stocks and crypto. We continuously monitor market moves, identify technical bottoms/tops and key opportunities, push high-value signals and intelligence via task workflows, and provide Financial AI Chat, stock analysis, and news/economic calendar to help you make better trading decisions.
